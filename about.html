About Me

Throughout my academic and personal projects, I’ve worked extensively with technologies like Node.js, React, Django, Spring Boot, MongoDB, and MySQL. My approach balances clean frontend design with powerful backend logic.

In addition to development, I have exposure to data analytics using Power BI and Python-based libraries, and enjoy solving DSA problems to sharpen my logic.
